Fried Chicken

Description

This crispy fried chicken recipe gives you perfectly juicy meat on the inside with a golden, crunchy coating on the outside. It's a comforting dish enjoyed all over the world, and perfect for family dinners, picnics, or casual get-togethers.

Our recipe uses a seasoned flour coating, and the chicken is fried to golden perfection. You can serve it with fries, coleslaw, or even waffles for that classic Southern touch!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (drumsticks or thighs)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place the chicken pieces in a bowl and pour in the buttermilk. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight for best flavor.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk and coat each piece in the seasoned flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Once hot, carefully add the chicken pieces in batches.
  5. Fry for about 10â€“15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the chicken and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
