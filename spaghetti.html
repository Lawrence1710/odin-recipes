Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This classic spaghetti bolognese is a hearty and flavorful Italian dish made with ground meat, tomatoes, and herbs. Perfect for dinner, it’s rich, comforting, and satisfying.

Serve it with grated Parmesan cheese and garlic bread for the ultimate experience.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Drain pasta and serve with the sauce. Top with cheese if desired.
