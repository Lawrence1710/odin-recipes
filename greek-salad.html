Greek Salad

Description

Greek salad is a fresh, colorful dish made with cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, olives, and feta cheese. It's light, healthy, and perfect as a side or light main course.

This Mediterranean staple is easy to prepare and full of bright, tangy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 150g feta cheese, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a large bowl.
  2. Add feta cheese on top.
  3. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  5. Serve immediately or chill for 15 minutes before serving.
